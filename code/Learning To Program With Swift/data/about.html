Infinite Skills is a video training publisher, founded with the goal in mind to provide high quality computer based training to consumers.

With offices in North America and distribution facilities around the world, Infinite Skills is a leading provider of software training solutions around the world. With high quality tutorials on the world's most widely used software and programming languages, the company seeks to push the boundaries of eLearning and interactive education.

Since Infinite Skills started creating it's own software training titles, we have published over 80 individual titles, ranging from mainstream programs such as Adobe Photoshop, to niche training such as Revit and Maya.

Infinite Skills has recently expanded into the tablet and smartphone sector with their iPad and iPhone Apps. In late 2011 Infinite Skills will publish their entire catalog for the iPad through their ground-breaking App, available right now in the Apple App store.

Infinite Skills continues to innovate and lead the market in new technology and training techniques, and will do so for many years to come.
