2-15, 2-14, 2-40 Life, Health & VA Online Course


Our online virtual classroom allows you unlimited interaction with teachers and fellow students, without the need to be in a certain place at a certain time.

60 Hrs 2-15 Life, Health, Annuities & Variable, DFS course ID 93665
40 Hrs 2-14 Life (Including Annuities & Variable Contracts), DFS course ID 90794
40 Hrs 2-40 Health, DFS course ID 90795

Our online course is custom designed by us for our students. No one else offers this course. You have an instructor available 7 days a week who monitors your progress, follows up with you, and answers your questions through the discussion forum, email, telephone, or in person. We provide new learning materials as needed by our students, just like in any other instructor-led classroom. For example, if you need a visual aid or a new example of a concept to help you understand it, we make up a new visual aid or come up with new examples.

This course covers Life, Health, Annuities and Variable Contracts. Passing this course and the state exam will make you eligible for either the 2-15, 2-14 or 2-40 Agent License.

The course has a 60 day time limit, STARTING THE DAY YOU REGISTER. If you need to delay the start, please let us know within 7 days of your registration.

Price: $175.00
