Teaching and Advising Insurance Professionals...
for Over 50 Years
Under Andy Beverly’s leadership we have provided quality insurance education statewide for more than 50 years. Over 80,000 students have been through the school’s various courses at last count.

Why attend the Andy Beverly School?
Does it matter who you do your pre-licensing education with? It does matter if you believe that a strong career in insurance starts with good information and good teaching. Florida Insurance School teachers make sure you have the tools you need for a great start in the insurance industry. You will certainly be prepared for your state exam; after all, our students have been passing longer than anyone else's. But you're also enrolling in a school that is interested in the success of this industry. Even if you call us 20 years after the last licensing class you take with us, we'll help you any way we can.

When and Where can I take classes?
Wondering when and where classes take place? The Courses & Materials link at the top of the page will take you to a list of in-class and online courses, books and study tools for licenses including the 2-20 General Lines Agent, the 20-44 Personal Lines Agent, and Life Health and Variable Annuities. Depending on demand, we may also add classes and locations throughout the year. If you have a group of students who need classes at your location, we can accommodate that as well.

Do you offer Online Classes?
We offer our own online classes and webinars. All of the webinars and most of the self-paced classes we offer are designed and produced by us. This ensures that you always have an experienced teacher to answer your questions. By giving you access to our own real, experienced teachers, we have bridged the gap between classroom courses and distance learning. Others attempt to save costs by automating their courses and not making teachers available. We have no interest in that. If you want to talk to a teacher on the phone, send an email, post in the Q&A forum or chat, even stop by the office for help, that's all part of what you get when you sign up for our online classes.

Here are a few other reasons our students consider online classes:

  • They can be less expensive because you have no travel or hotel costs.
  • You can learn from the comfort of your home or office.
  • You can learn at your own pace, whether faster or slower than a classroom course.
  • You can always reach a knowledgeable teacher when you need help, and a teacher will check on your progress periodically.
  • Start the course when you want: no waiting for scheduled classes.
  • Complete reading assignments from anywhere: a park, the beach, a coffee shop.
  • High student satisfaction! Competing self-paced courses offer no help from teachers and make learning difficult. We do everything we can to send you fully prepared into the industry.
